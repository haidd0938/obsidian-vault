## Pitfalls to Avoid

1. **Overselling risky services** — ChatGPT Plus accounts DO get banned. Don't promise lifetime.
2. **Ignoring payment methods** — Many edu benefits need a foreign credit card. The user needs one.
3. **Overlooking language barriers** — Some verification processes are in English. Flag this.
4. **Assuming all edu emails work everywhere** — Some services check email domain quality.
5. **Forgetting about proxy/VPN** — Many US services don't work from China without one.
6. **Not accounting for edu email lifespan** — Cheap emails may get revoked in months.
7. **Recommending high-touch services for low-time users** — Escrow/delivery arbitrage is manual.
